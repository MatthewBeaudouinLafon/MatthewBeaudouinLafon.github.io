The Design Nature Play Project

Design Nature is one of the first classes offered at Olin. One of the projects involves designing a play experience for fourth graders that is inspired by an animal. We chose the Alligator because we had a pretty good idea for a game that could be played. Changed our minds. Went with this.

Design

The idea was to recreate the alligator's "Death Roll", because we figured fourth graders would like to roll around.

When the day came to present our game to fourth graders, it became clear very quickly that doing on-the-fly cost-benefit analysis was perhaps asking a tad too much from the children. We still wanted the game to involve more than just rolling around, so we used each group of student as a test group for slightly different game mechanics. Sometimes have them look inside the 'dirbs', during the game, sometimes after, sometimes not at all.
